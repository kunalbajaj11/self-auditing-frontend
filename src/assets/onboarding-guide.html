SelfAccounting.AI — Client Onboarding Checklist

First-time setup guide for new organizations • Estimated time: 30–60 minutes

Overview

Welcome to SelfAccounting.AI. This checklist guides you through the essential setup steps so your organization can start tracking expenses, managing invoices, and running reports effectively.

You will need:

  • Your license key (provided by your account manager)
  • Company registration details (name, VAT number, address)
  • Admin user details (name, email, password)
  • Bank account details (for invoices and reconciliation)
  • List of key vendors and customers (optional for initial setup)

Part 1: Account Registration

Step 1: Register with License Key

  1. Go to the registration page (Get Started or Register with License).
  2. Enter your license key and click Validate.
  3. Complete the registration form:

4. Click Register. You will be logged in and a welcome email will be sent.

What happens automatically:

  • Organization is created
  • Default expense categories (Fuel, Food, Utilities, Travel, etc.) are created
  • Admin user is created and linked to the organization
  • License is activated

Part 2: Essential Configuration (Do First)

Step 2: Complete Company Profile

Settings → Organization Settings (or /admin/company)

  • Company Name — Verify and update if needed
  • VAT Number (TRN) — Required for UAE tax invoices
  • Currency — Confirm base currency
  • Primary Contact — Name of main contact
  • Contact Email — Email for invoices and correspondence
  • Registered Address — Full address for invoices and legal documents
  • Bank Account Details — Bank name, account holder, IBAN, Swift, branch (for invoices)

Why: This information is printed on sales invoices and used in reports. Incomplete details can cause compliance issues.

Step 3: Tax Settings

Settings → Tax Settings (or /admin/settings/tax)

  • Add standard VAT rate (e.g., 5% for UAE)
  • Add zero-rated and exempt rules if applicable
  • Ensure rates match your TRN and local regulations

Step 4: Invoice Template

Settings → Invoice Template

  • Upload your company logo
  • Set invoice footer (terms, payment instructions)
  • Configure branding (colours, layout if supported)

Step 5: Numbering Sequences

Settings → Numbering Sequences

  • Set invoice number prefix (e.g., INV-2025-)
  • Set expense/receipt reference format if needed

Part 3: Master Data Setup

Step 6: Expense Categories

Settings → Expense Categories

Default categories are created automatically. Review, add, or edit as needed.

Step 7: Expense Types

Settings → Expense Types

Review system defaults (OPEX, CAPEX) and add custom types if needed.

Step 8: Ledger Accounts

Settings → Ledger Accounts

Review or import your chart of accounts. Ensure core accounts exist: Revenue, Expenses, Bank, Receivables, Payables, VAT.

Step 9: Bank Accounts & Cash Accounts

Banking → Bank Accounts / Cash Accounts

Add each bank account and petty cash accounts if used.

Part 4: Operational Setup

Step 10: Vendors

Contacts → Vendors

Add your main vendors/suppliers with name, contact, and TRN (if VAT registered).

Step 11: Customers

Contacts → Customers

Add customers before creating sales invoices. Include name, contact, TRN, and payment terms.

Step 12: User Management

Settings → User Management

Invite team members and assign roles: Admin, Accountant, Employee.
