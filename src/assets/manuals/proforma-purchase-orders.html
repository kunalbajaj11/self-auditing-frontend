Proforma Invoices & Purchase Orders

Proforma (quotes) and purchase order workflow

Proforma Invoices

A proforma is a quote or draft sent before a sale is final—not a legal request for payment. Create as Proforma, send PDF to customer, then Convert to Tax Invoice when they confirm. Record payment when received.

Purchase Orders (PO)

A PO is your commitment to a vendor. Create PO (Expenses → Purchase Order), send to vendor, receive goods, then when the vendor invoice arrives create an Expense and link to the PO. Match amounts and close the PO.

PO Statuses

Draft → Sent → Acknowledged → Partially received → Fully received → Invoiced → Closed (or Cancelled).
