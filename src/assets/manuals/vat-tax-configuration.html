VAT & Tax Configuration Guide

Configure VAT rates and tax settings for UAE

Where to Configure

Settings → Tax Settings. Your organization TRN (VAT number) is in Settings → Organization Settings.

Tax Rates

Add standard rate (e.g. 5% UAE), zero-rated (0%), and exempt. These drive invoice and expense VAT and the VAT Control Account report.

Invoices and Expenses

Select the correct VAT rate per invoice line or expense. Vendor TRN can be used for reverse charge. Use Reports → VAT Control Account to verify output vs input VAT.
