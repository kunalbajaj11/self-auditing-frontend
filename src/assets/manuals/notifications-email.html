Notifications & Email Settings

Reminders, alerts, and email in SelfAccounting.AI

Where to View Notifications

Settings → Email / Notifications (Reminders & Alerts). View list, mark as read, open related expense or invoice.

What You Receive

  • Welcome email after registration or when an admin creates your user.
  • Password reset email with link (expires after a short period).
  • Reminders (if enabled): e.g. pending approvals, overdue invoices, compliance deadlines.

Email Not Arriving

Check spam/junk; allowlist the sender (e.g. support@selfaccounting.ai). Confirm your email in profile. Contact support if needed—SMTP is configured at platform level.
