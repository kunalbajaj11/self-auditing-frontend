Receipt & OCR Guide

Upload receipts and get the best results from automatic data extraction

Supported Formats

Images (JPEG, PNG) and PDF. Upload via the expense form or Upload Expense flow.

How to Upload

When creating or editing an expense, use the Attachments/Receipt section. Click Upload or drag and drop. If OCR is enabled, vendor, date, amount (and sometimes VAT) are auto-filled. Review and correct before saving.

Better OCR Results

  • Clear, well-lit photos or scans; avoid blur and shadows.
  • Crop to the receipt only; keep text right-side up.
  • PDFs from email or apps often work well.
  • Handwritten or faded text may need manual entry.

When OCR Fails

You can still attach the file and enter all details manually. The attachment remains for audit.
