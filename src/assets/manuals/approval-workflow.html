Approval Workflow Guide

How expense approval works and how to approve or reject

Overview

Employees submit expenses; Approvers, Admins, and Accountants can view pending expenses and approve or reject them. Approved expenses can then be paid.

Viewing Pending Approvals

Go to Expenses → Expenses, filter by status Pending approval. Open an expense to see details and receipt.

Approving

Open the expense → review amount, vendor, category, attachment → click Approve. Status changes to approved.

Rejecting

Open the expense → click Reject. Enter a reason if prompted. Submitter can edit and resubmit.

After Approval

Approved expenses appear in reports and can be selected for payment (Expenses → Payments).
