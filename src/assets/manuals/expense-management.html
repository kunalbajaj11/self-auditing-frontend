Expense Management Guide

Create, edit, and manage expenses; categories; receipts; vendors and POs

Where to Manage Expenses

Admin / Accountant: Expenses → Expenses. Employee: Upload Expense or My Expenses (own expenses only).

Creating an Expense

  1. Go to Expenses → Expenses (or Upload Expense / My Expenses).
  2. Click Add Expense.
  3. Fill in: Vendor (select or enter), Category, Expense type, Amount, Date, Description, VAT (often auto), Attachments (receipt).
  4. Optionally link to a Purchase Order. Save.

Categories and Expense Types

Settings → Expense Categories — e.g. Fuel, Travel, Utilities. Settings → Expense Types — OPEX, CAPEX; link to ledger accounts.
