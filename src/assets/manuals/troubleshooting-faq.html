Troubleshooting & FAQ

Common issues and quick fixes

Login & Account

I forgot my password.

Use Forgot Password on the login page. Check spam for the reset link. Request again if it expired.

"User limit reached" when inviting.

Standard plan = 5 users. Upgrade or deactivate a user who no longer needs access.

Expenses & Receipts

OCR didn't extract data.

Upload a clearer image or enter details manually. See Receipt & OCR Guide.

Expense stuck in "Pending approval".

An Approver, Accountant, or Admin must Approve or Reject. See Approval Workflow.

Invoices & VAT

Invoice total or VAT wrong.

Check Settings → Tax Settings and Organization TRN. See VAT & Tax.

Bank & Import

Don't see "Bulk Import" for journal entries.

Bulk import is enabled per organization. Contact your account manager to enable Migration.
