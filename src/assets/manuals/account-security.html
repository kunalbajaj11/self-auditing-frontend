Account & Security Guide

Passwords, login, and best practices

Logging In

Sign In from the homepage. Enter email and password. You are taken to your workspace (Admin, Accountant, or Employee) by role.

Passwords

Minimum 8 characters. Use a strong, unique password. Do not share it. Change it periodically or if exposed.

Forgot Password

On the login page click Forgot Password. Enter your email. Check inbox (and spam) for the reset link. Link expires after a short time; request again if needed. Set new password and log in.

Sign Out

Use Sign out in the app when done, especially on shared devices. Sessions may expire after inactivity.

Best Practices

  • Strong, unique password; change if compromised.
  • Do not share password; sign out on shared computers.
  • Allowlist SelfAccounting.AI email so reset and alerts are not missed.
  • Report suspicious activity to admin or support.
