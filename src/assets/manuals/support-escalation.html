Support & Escalation

How to get help and report issues

Self-Service First

Use the FAQ & Manuals page (from the homepage) for setup, expenses, invoices, reports, tax, migration, and troubleshooting.

Contact Support

Email: support@selfaccounting.ai

Phone: +971-58-900-3150

Use the channel provided with your license or by your account manager.

When Reporting an Issue, Include

  1. What you were doing (e.g. creating expense, uploading statement)
  2. What happened (error message, wrong result)
  3. What you expected
  4. Your role (Admin, Accountant, Employee)
  5. Browser and device
  6. Screenshot or exact error text (remove sensitive data)

For Your Admin

User and access issues: contact your organization's Admin (User Management). Billing and license: contact your account manager.
